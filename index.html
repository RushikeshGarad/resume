Our Story

Discover the heritage behind SilkSutra

Weaving Dreams Since 1995

Founded by textile connoisseur Meera Patel, SilkSutra began as a humble boutique with a vision to preserve India's rich textile heritage while making it relevant for the modern woman.

Today, we collaborate with over 200 master weavers across India to bring you the finest handcrafted sarees that blend timeless tradition with contemporary elegance.

Each SilkSutra creation tells a story - of the skilled hands that wove it, the heritage it represents, and the modern woman who brings it to life.
